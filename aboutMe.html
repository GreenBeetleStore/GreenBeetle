My story, a few words about me...

My profile summarized ...

I have a rich and varied background that has broadened and diversified my experiences.

· My disability has given me self-confidence, rigor, perseverance, inventiveness, a sense of challenge and a lot of determination.
· The creation of companies has given me autonomy, independence, versatility, self-learning, initiative and innovation.
· The specialization as a mountain guide made me meticulous, analytical, foresight, pedagogic, empathetic, committed to teamwork, and taught me to manage groups with different particularities.
· My last experience, and my professional retraining, testify to my professional awareness, my motivation and my adaptability in a job that fascinates me.

It opens me up to new horizons and challenges!

My personal journey ...

One's character and personality are forged as we grow up, the result of the education we receive, our cultural environment, our relationships, our experiences and above all our will.
The entire life cycle of an individual will influence the modularity and evolution of our way of being and our human competencies.
A personal journey, varied and rich in experiences together with the will of oneself, provides us with a wide range of possibilities to choose how we are.
