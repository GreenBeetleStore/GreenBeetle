Requirements

Objectives :

  • Have the basic knowledge to move freely and safely in the canyons.
  • Abilities to pass the entrance test for a medium-level sports technician in canyons.

Contents :

  • Organization and forecast : Equipment and material.
  • Techniques of progression without rope :
    • March : approach, during and return of a canyon. Choice of the correct itinerary.
    • Swimming : safety position.
    • Diving : recovery of lost objects …
    • Jump : jumping techniques.
    • Slide : slide techniques.
    • Descent and climbing : techniques of ascent and descent in different terrains.
  • Rope progression techniques :
    • Rappelling manoeuvres : classic rappelling, rapid rappelling, double brake rappelling, disengageable rappelling, adjusted rappelling, guided rappelling.
    • Self-belay maneuvers : from above (dynamic knot) and from below.
    • Unmissable eight technique.
    • Handrail progression : without intermediate points, with intermediate points.
  • Knots (dynamic, clove hitch, flat, butterfly, eight, nine, etc.)

Access Tests :

Access tests to the second level of the middle grade.

· First part

(common for all the specialties of mountain and climbing sports).

  • Objective : assessment of physical condition.
  • Content :
    • Trekking, in the time established by the court and carrying a backpack with a minimum of 10 kilograms of continuous weight, a mountain itinerary of a minimum of 12 kilometers in varied terrain, with 1,500 meters of accumulated positive slope.
    • Dexterity, after the accumulated effort of the trekking, carry out 3 workshops of different timed routes. One over dense grassy terrain, another over dense gravel terrain and another overcoming obstacles with large rocks.
    • Evaluation, this first part will be passed when the applicant has completed the itineraries in the times established by the court.

· Second part

(specific to the canyoning specialty).

  • Objective : assessment of specific skills.
  • Content :
    • Climbing : Complete an equipped route, of V degree of difficulty, of a minimum length of 20 meters, in the time established by the court. In the event of a fall, the applicant must start the itinerary again from the place where it started, acting as the first roped.
    • Swimming : Swim 100 meters in calm or live water, wearing a 7 mm neoprene suit and carrying the necessary material for progression in canyons in the harness, in a determined time.
    • Diving : Dive to a depth of 3 meters, using the appropriate technique, wearing a full 7 mm neoprene suit and carrying the necessary material for progression in canyons in the harness.
    • Canyoning : Carry out a canyoning descent on varied terrain independently, including walking, "downclimbing" and "conventional rappelling", in the time established by the court.
    • Evaluation : this second part will be passed when the applicant has:
      · Completed the on-sight climbing tour in the established time.
      · Surface swam the required distance in the allotted time.
      · Dive without swimming, on the first attempt, without jumping and using the correct technical gesture.
      · Finished the descent of the canyon in the established time, without having suffered more than three falls.
      · To this end, it will be taken into account that each exit from the marked circuit will be penalized by increasing the time obtained by the applicant by 5 per 100 of the time established by the court.
      · Correctly used the specific technical material of each sports specialty, without using other materials than the specific ones and in sufficient quantity.

The Personal Improvement ...

PERSONAL IMPROVEMENT IMPOSES A CHANGE THROUGH WHICH A PERSON ACQUIRES A SERIES OF QUALITIES THAT IMPROVE HIS LIFE.

It is about seeking and finding a state of satisfaction with ourselves and the circumstances around us.

But, a process of change causes fear and insecurity, mainly due to deeply rooted erroneous beliefs. It is difficult to banish these beliefs and move beyond fear.

Many people complain about what they have in their lives, but do nothing to change it. Every day they demonstrate their dissatisfaction, but also their immobility. If you are not happy with what surrounds you, it is time to move and not stop.

Don't settle, don't stop looking, let curiosity guide your days. Remember how you explored the world when you were a child and recover this way of seeing life, of living an adventure every second, without stopping.

When we face a new challenge it seems very complicated, full of uncertainty and stresses us, but this is the barrier we have to overcome to be who we want to be and not what others expect of us.

Over time, what was initially difficult becomes easy because we learn to overcome our fears, to assume them and to face them. We recognize them and face them, manage them and they cease to be a threat to become a motivation.

We often tend to give priority to other people, we turn ourselves into others, what they want and what they need, by making them happy, sometimes paying the price of our own happiness. Where are we ?

It's time to become your own priority, to know that sometimes you have to be ahead, that you need some time for yourself. It's not about being selfish, it's about thinking in a balanced way about others and yourself. You deserve to be happy.

We deserve to be happy and sometimes we forget that. We tend to do what we think makes others happy and we don't reflect on what we are passionate about, what fills our hearts and makes us smile.

You also deserve to enjoy happiness, you deserve to have your heart and soul full of joy, to squeeze every moment, to have dreams fulfilled and to fulfill, to transmit your passion and to feel loved. You are brave and take risks.

The biggest obstacle when it comes to changing and overcoming ourselves is fear, because there is always a risk. Managing this fear without avoiding it, simply facing it, is what makes us learn and grow in life.

In order to grow as people, we will always have to take risks and not forget that in our bet there is also a risk of failure, but this failure will be a learning experience, a way to get to know each other better and to know how to react to fears .

In this life it is not possible to fully realize everything we can wish for, but if you follow your instincts and your heart you will see that new opportunities always appear according to the circumstances, take advantage of them !

If you don't try, you'll never know what you're capable of, so do it !
